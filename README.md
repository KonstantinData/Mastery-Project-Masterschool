# Mastery Project - The TravelTide Company 

**Author:** Konstantin Milonas  

---

## Welcome to the Mastery Project!

This project simulates a real-world business scenario where you take on the role of a data analyst supporting the Marketing team at **TravelTide**, an innovative e-booking startup. Your objective: provide actionable insights to personalize a new customer **rewards program**.

Using data-driven methods, you'll analyze user behavior, engineer meaningful features, and segment customers based on their preferences. The outcome? A marketing strategy that speaks directly to customer interests—boosting engagement and retention.

---

## 🏢 The Business Context: TravelTide

TravelTide, founded in April 2021, is a fast-growing startup known for offering the **largest travel inventory** in the e-booking industry. While the platform excels in data aggregation and search, it struggles with **customer retention**.

To solve this, **Elena Tarrant**, TravelTide’s new Head of Marketing, is launching a rewards program and needs support from the analytics team to determine:
- Which **perks** are most attractive to customers
- How to **assign customers to segments** based on preferences

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The main goals:
1. Validate the hypothesis that customers have different preferred rewards.
2. Assign each customer the **most relevant perk** to personalize the email campaign.
3. Support the marketing strategy with **clear customer segmentation and data storytelling**.

---

## 🗂️ Project Structure

### Week 1: Exploration & Feature Engineering
- Understand the business goals and available datasets.
- Clean and join data from different sources (e.g. session data, customer metadata).
- Generate new metrics and features to better describe customer behavior.

### Week 2: Segmentation & Strategy
- Use the engineered features to segment customers.
- Apply clustering algorithms and analyze group characteristics.
- Assign a tailored perk to each segment.
- Create a compelling **presentation** with data visualizations and business recommendations.

---

## 🧠 Key Skills Applied

- **Data Cleaning** and Preparation  
- **Feature Engineering**  
- **Customer Segmentation** using **Machine Learning** (e.g. Clustering)  
- **Visualization** & **Data Storytelling**  
- **Strategic Business Recommendations**  
- Tools: SQL, Python, Google Sheets, Tableau


---

## 📩 Example Output

Elena shared two versions of the customer email. One version emphasizes **Free Cancellation**, while the other lists all perks equally. Your job is to validate which **messaging strategy** works best by identifying which customer would respond best to which perk.

**Potential Perks:**
- Free hotel meal  
- Free checked bag  
- No cancellation fees  
- Exclusive discounts  
- One night free hotel with flight  

---

## 📊 Deliverables

- Cleaned and aggregated customer data
- Feature matrix for segmentation
- Customer segments with assigned perks
- Final slide presentation with visuals and strategy
- Recommendations for how TravelTide can **track the impact** of the campaign
